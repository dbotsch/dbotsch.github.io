What is Bling?

Bling is a group of dancers within the Centrestage Passion Company. This company is dedicated to the artform know as competitive dance. Members of this company spend counteless hours each week training, rehearsing, and performing around the state and even nationally!

Who is Bling?

Bling dancers are a handpicked group of intermediate/advanced dancers who have shown dedication and a love for the sport. Each year, dancers audition for a placement on the Centrestage Passion Company, such as the Bling Company. This year, the competitive dance company is comprised of 19 dancers aging from 8 to 11 years old. These dancers will grow as a team throughout the season and offer support and a competitive edge to one another.
